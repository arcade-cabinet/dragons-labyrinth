Hex E6S32 in Black Shield Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Black Shield Timberlands > Hex E6S32

Carved into the base of a titanic tree, the Citadel of Hoviir is a truly breath-taking sight. Banners of woven vines decorate the towers, and guards clad in polished emerald armour patrol the battlements.

There's a crowded fighting pit near a water stream. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

2,008 gp will be awarded to the winner.
