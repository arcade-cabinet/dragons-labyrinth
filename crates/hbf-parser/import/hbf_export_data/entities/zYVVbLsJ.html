Description
Broken vials and beakers are scattered all over the place and the ground is slick with slime. There’s also a colourful glow emitting from the ground.
  • A few blue, red and yellow bioluminescent mushrooms are growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming them will be paralyzed with mind twisting hallucinations for 1d6 turns.
  • Dried flowers litter the area.
  • Searching will uncover a nook in the wall holding the key (🜏) to unlock the round bronze door (to the N) in area 14
  • There’s a rope-pull lever that can open the secret door in area 22 (to the E)
