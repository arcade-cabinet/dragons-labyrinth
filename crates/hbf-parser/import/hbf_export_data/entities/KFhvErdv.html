Doorways
  • S side - rectangular bronze door (Stuck)
  • W side - triangle wooden door (Barricaded)
  • E side - passage to area 33.
Description
Preserved organs in glass jars are placed inside wall cavities and the ceiling is damp with moisture. Faint colourful light is emitting from everywhere.
  • There are a few dozen colourful bioluminescent mushrooms growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming these will suffer a severe stomach ache and a -2 penalty to attack rolls for 1d6 turns.
  • A smashed lyre can be found below an arch.
  • Searching will uncover a cloth wrap under a stone holding the key (🝯) to unlock the odd-shaped bronze door (to the N) in area 72
