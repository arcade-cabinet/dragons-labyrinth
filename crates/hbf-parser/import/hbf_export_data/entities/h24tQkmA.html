Description
A decorated ceremonial stone was placed here and the hidden alcoves are covered with rusty stains. There is a big jar near the far wall.
  • The jar is trapped. There's a small gas-filled blister made of rat's intestine embedded in a handle. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jar contains:
    • 217 gp in coins
    • 600 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): 2 × jasper, 2 × Bloodstone, Carnelian, Chalcedony, 2 × Chrysoprase, Zircon, 3 × Citrine
    • Magic Items: Potion of heroism, Scroll5th of Dream (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a pile of bones in the far corner and some feathers next to it.
