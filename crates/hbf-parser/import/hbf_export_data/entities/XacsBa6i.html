Description
You hear the sound of water dripping down and the stalagmites have several burn marks on them. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a poisonous gas trap here. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if removing a specific object from its original place, causing the gas to come out of slits in the wall. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting orc corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 35 gp in coins
    • 200 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): 2 × Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, Carved bone statuette, Embroidered silk handkerchief, Pair of engraved bone dice, 3 × Silver ewer
    • Magic Items: Bag of beans, 2 × Oil of etherealness, Potion of clairvoyance
    • Shortsword (Usable)
    • Garlic (Unusable)
    • Pole (10’ long, wooden) (Unusable)
  • Many candles, burnt out in a pile of wax can be found in the near corner.
