Doorways
  • N side - odd-shaped wooden door (Stuck)
  • S side - arched wooden door (Trapped)
    • This doorway is magically trapped. When undetected using a DC18 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger when crossing. Succeed a DC20 Intelligence saving throw or teleport to a random area.

Description
Large symbols and letters were painted all over the place and the entryway is carved with claw marks. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a deadly pit here, 6' deep, hidden under canvas and dirt. If undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, there's a chance of stepping into it. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or fall in and sustain 2d6 damage from the long rusty spikes at the bottom.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of an orc. Searching it will uncover:
    • 56 gp in coins
    • Magic Items: Potion of resistance, Potion of water breathing
    • Mace (Unusable)
    • Pole (10’ long, wooden) (Usable)
    • Oil (Usable)
    • The key (🝦) to unlock the round wooden door (to the N) in area 22
  • There’s also a torn cloak in the near corner and some dried flowers next to it.
