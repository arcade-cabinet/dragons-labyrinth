Foreshadowing
  • There's a good chance players could sense the vile stench that the Gray Oozes are emitting from inside this area.
Description
Pools of dark sludge are scattered all over the place and the stalactites are scorched with burn marks. A strange sensation is creeping up your spine.
  • There are 2 Gray Oozes inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.
