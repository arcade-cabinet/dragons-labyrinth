A small pond sits between the jungle trees here. At night, countless mysterious forms glow an eerie purple light deep in the water.

Two adventurers are crouching and whispering where the jungle meets a tall mountain. Berlinde and Huldward are arguing about how best to deal with the monsters blocking the path ahead (use the random encounters table below). Berlinde thinks they should fight it, whilst their companion feels sneaking by would be safer.
