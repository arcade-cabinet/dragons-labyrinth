Hex E2S53 in Heartseeker Moors (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Heartseeker Moors > Hex E2S53

To walk here one must submerge deep in the swamp and suffer not only the wet and mud, but the occasional leech and water centipede.

An improvised but crowded fighting arena stands on a dry patch of land. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

2,232 gp will be awarded to the winner.
