Doorways

Description
Broken vials and beakers are scattered all over the place and the hidden alcoves have several ancient carvings on them. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 18 gp in coins
    • Shortbow (Usable)
    • Rope (50’) (Unusable)
    • The key (🜋) to unlock the rectangular wooden door (to the N) in area 3
  • There’s also a torn quiver near the wall on the right and some cube shaped stones next to it.
  • Searching will uncover a goat’s skull holding the key (🝛) to unlock the rectangular bronze door (to the E) in area 84
