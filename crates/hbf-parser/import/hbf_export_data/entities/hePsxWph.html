Hex E2S28 in Goblinchaser Jungle (The Lands of Vo'il)

Colossal Footprints > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goblinchaser Jungle > Hex E2S28

This part of the jungle has a very dense undergrowth of bushes and vines. A sword or a machete is likely needed to carve the way forward.

Colossal footprints mark the ground deep in the jungle. The imprints could fit an entire wagon within.
