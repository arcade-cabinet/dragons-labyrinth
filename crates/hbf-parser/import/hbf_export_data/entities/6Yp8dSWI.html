Vast plateaus of ice stretch in all directions, feature-less and monotonous. Navigating is difficult without any distant landmarks.

A field of carved boulders stretches on the icy plateau. like an abandoned sculpture garden. Each rock has been carved into a face, the features exaggerated and distorted like a disturbing reflection.

A Stone Giant wanders the boulder field, ambushing those who come to investigate.
