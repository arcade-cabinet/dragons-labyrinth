This area stinks from excrements and the cavern walls have several rusty stains on them. There are some large bags near the far wall.
  • The bags are trapped. There's a poisoned needle thrown casually inside. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the bags contain:
    • 117 gp in coins
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a carved idol near the wall on the far end and some shattered pieces of glass next to it.
