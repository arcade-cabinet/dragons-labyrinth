Doorways
  • S side - round wooden door (Barricaded)
Description
Torn and bloody canvas curtains partition the area and the hidden alcoves are covered with claw marks. There’s also a colourful glow emitting from the ground.
  • A few dozen green and purple bioluminescent mushrooms are growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming them will suffer a severe stomach ache and a -2 penalty to attack rolls for 1d6 turns.
  • There’s also an eyeball in a sealed jar on an altar and some small stones with runes etchings spread all over the place.
