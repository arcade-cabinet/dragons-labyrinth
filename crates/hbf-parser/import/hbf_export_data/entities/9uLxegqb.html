Description
The corridor here is damp and slippery from sludge.
Trap!
There's a deadly pit here, 6' deep, covered under thin wood planks. If undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, there's a chance of stepping into it. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or fall in and sustain 2d6 damage from the long rusty spikes at the bottom.
