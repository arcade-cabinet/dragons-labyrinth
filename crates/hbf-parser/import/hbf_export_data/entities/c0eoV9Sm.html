Hex E5S45 in Heartseeker Forest (The Lands of Vo'il)

Ancient Artifact > > The Lands of Vo'il > Heartseeker Forest > Hex E5S45

The sallow trees bow and scrape, dusty leaves brushing the ancient, over-grown road below. A way stone—weathered beyond recognition—marks the distance to a long-gone settlement.

When exploring this area, a giant triangular artifact made of polished iron, with large pointy spears attached, can be found wedged deep in the woods.

There's a 1-in-6 chance to find a secret door leading inside the artifact and a successful INT check will allow using a contraption inside it.

If used, the object will magically levitate and will travel 1d6 hexes in a matter of minutes before crashing into the ground again, dealing 1d6 damage to anyone inside.
