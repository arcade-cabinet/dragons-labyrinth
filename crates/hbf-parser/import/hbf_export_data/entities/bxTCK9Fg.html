Foreshadowing
  • A few single copper pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
It is dark and damp here and the ground is carved with claw marks. There are some very big jars near the far wall.
  • The jars are trapped. There's a poisoned needle embedded in a handle. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jars contain:
    • 193 gp in coins
    • 400 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Onyx, Bloodstone, 2 × Carnelian, 2 × Chrysoprase, Star rose quartz, Citrine
    • Magic Items: Bag of holding, Potion of fire breath, Potion of poison, Potion of resistance
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a blanket, laid out and covering something small in the far corner and some small mounds of ashes spread all over the place.
