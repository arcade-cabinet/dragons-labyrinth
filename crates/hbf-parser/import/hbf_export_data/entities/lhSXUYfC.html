Foreshadowing
  • A few single copper pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Doorways
  • S side - rectangular wooden door (Trapped)
    • There's a giant blade locked inside a cavity above the door. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through an almost invisible trip wire. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • E side - odd-shaped wooden door (Stuck)
Description
Smashed wooden boxes litter boundaries and the entryway is slick with slime. There is a small jar near the far wall.
  • The jar is trapped. There's a small gas-filled vial made of an extremely fragile glass embedded in a handle. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jar contains:
    • 128 gp in coins
    • 75 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, Cloth-of-gold vestments, Gold locket with a painted portrait inside
    • Magic Items: Oil of etherealness, Periapt of health, Potion of clairvoyance, Potion of stone giant strength
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a pile of bones near the wall on the far end and some brightly colored leaves spread all over the place.
  • There’s a crank lever that can open the secret door in area 3 (to the W)
  • The words "Miraculous Soul" in Gargoyle language are graffitied on the ceiling. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 34 (to the W)
