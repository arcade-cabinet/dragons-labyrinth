Hex E6S53 in Hell's Gate Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Hell's Gate Desert > Hex E6S53

There are numerous sinkholes in the sands here. Some of them are big enough to swallow an inn.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found beside the ruins of a pyramid.
