Description
Various torturing devices are fixtured here and the ceiling is blackened. There is a large jar near the far wall.
  • The jar is trapped. Loose stones are locked above the jar, released when a switch embedded in a handle is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jar contains:
    • 218 gp in coins
    • 600 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Onyx, Sardonyx, jasper, 3 × Bloodstone, 3 × Carnelian, Chalcedony, Citrine, Quartz
    • Magic Items: 2 × Potion of invisibility, 2 × Potion of supreme healing, Sling bullets (+3)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a pile of papers near the wall on the right and some dry remains of food spread all over the place.
