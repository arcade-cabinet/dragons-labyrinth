Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Fallen Star Steppe > Hex E1S57

Carcasses of farm animals are scattered around, some in worse shape than others. Swarms of flies are hovering over them like dark veils in the wind.

An improvised but crowded fighting arena stands between two hills. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules

  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone
