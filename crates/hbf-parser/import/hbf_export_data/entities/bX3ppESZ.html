Moss covered ruins of an ancient stone structure emerge from within the vegetation. Carvings of spirals and swirls decorate some of the larger stones.

Two adventurers are crouching and whispering where the jungle meets a tall mountain. Helmward and Aleria are arguing about how best to deal with the monsters blocking the path ahead (use the random encounters table below). Helmward thinks they should fight it, whilst their companion feels sneaking by would be safer.
