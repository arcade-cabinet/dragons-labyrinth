Cave area #11 in Hideout of the Corrupted Order

Cave Dig > > Hideout of the Corrupted Order > Area # 11

Description
Oversized fossilised mushrooms are covering this area and the canopy is covered with cracks. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • There's a spring-loaded spears trap here, hidden inside shafts in the floor. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through its trip wire. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the violent thrust of the jumping spears.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 36 gp in coins
    • Shortsword (Unusable)
    • Rations (iron, 7 days) (Usable)
    • Silver Dagger (Unusable)
  • A smashed lyre can be found near the wall on the right.
