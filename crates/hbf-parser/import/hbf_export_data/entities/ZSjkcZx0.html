Description
There are stains of what seems to be blood on the ground and there are a few ancient carvings on the flowstones. There’s a dead body here.
  • There's a crushing swinging boulder trap here, going back and forth periodically. Succeed a DC15 Strengh (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) to avoid getting hit when passing it, or sustain 2d6 damage.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting goblin corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 89 gp in coins
    • 110 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Obsidian, Hematite, Turquoise, Rhodochrosite, Azurite, Malachite, Tiger eye, Eye agate, Moss agate, 2 × Blue quartz
    • Thieves’ tools (Usable)
  • There are also several iron bars, each about a foot in length near the wall on the left and some rotting remains of food spread all over the place.
