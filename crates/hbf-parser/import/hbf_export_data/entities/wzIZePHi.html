Hex W1S49 in Aurora Bushes (The Lands of Vo'il)

A Moving Contraption > > The Lands of Vo'il > Aurora Bushes > Hex W1S49

This part of the jungle has a very dense undergrowth of bushes and vines. A sword or a machete is likely needed to carve the way forward.

A great contraption rumbles where the jungle meets a tall mountain. Its thunderous report shaking the ground as it travels, massive wheels carving furrows in the ground.
