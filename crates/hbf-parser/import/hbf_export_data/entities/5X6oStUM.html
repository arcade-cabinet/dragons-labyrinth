Hex E8S45 in Goldenswan Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Portal > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldenswan Timberlands > Hex E8S45

So big and tall are the trees here. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

There’s a magical portal to another hex deep in the woods. The portal is a large slab of dark rock that has a seemingly hollow arch-shaped stone gate on top. There’s a strange purple glow surrounding it. Anyone or anything passing through the gate will get teleported into a different hex (preselected by the referee), but there’s a 1-in-6 chance that the portal will give the character donkey ears (roll per character).
