Hex E3 in Vicious Crags (The Lands of Vo'il)

Field of Boulders > > The Lands of Vo'il > Vicious Crags > Hex E3

Multiple plateaus separated by seemingly bottomless chasms are connected with fragile rope bridges. The strong wind swings the brides from side to side, making crossing them a terrifying experience.

A field of carved boulders stretches near the base of the tallest mountain. like an abandoned sculpture garden. Each rock has been carved into a face, the features exaggerated and distorted like a disturbing reflection.

A Stone Golem wanders the boulder field, ambushing those who come to investigate.
