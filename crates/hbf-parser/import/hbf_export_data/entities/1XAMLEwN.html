Description
This area is dark and cold and the floor has a few spots of green rot on it. Something here makes the hair at the back of the neck stand.
  • There are 2 Wights inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.

    • Life Drain: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Inflicts 5 (1d6 + 2) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC13 Constitution saving throw or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0., A humanoid slain by this attack rises 24 hours later as a TAG:creature under the wight's control, unless the humanoid is restored to life or its body is destroyed. The wight can have no more than twelve zombies under its control at one time.
    • Longsword: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Inflicts 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage, or 7 (1d10 + 2) slashing damage if used with two hands.
