Description
A decorated ceremonial stone was placed here and the floor is covered with spots of green rot. There is a small crate near the far wall.
  • The crate is trapped. There's a small gas-filled blister made of lizard's intestine embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 254 gp in coins
    • 125 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Cloth-of-gold vestments, 2 × Embroidered silk handkerchief, Gold locket with a painted portrait inside, Small mirror set in a painted wooden frame
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a stagnant puddle near the wall on the far end and some small iron cages next to it.
