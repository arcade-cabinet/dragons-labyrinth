Hex E1S28 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Colossal Footprints > > The Lands of Vo'il > Iceborn Wilderness > Hex E1S28

A huge, lush tree is standing in a clearing here, covered with colourful flowers and holding a sweet berry inside. Birds fly cheerfully around it.

Colossal footprints mark the ground where the jungle meets a tall mountain. The imprints could fit an entire wagon within.
