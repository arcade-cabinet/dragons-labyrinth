Hex E7S35 in Heartseeker Forest (The Lands of Vo'il)

Ancient Well > > The Lands of Vo'il > Heartseeker Forest > Hex E7S35

Moss covered ruins of an ancient stone structure emerge from within the vegetation. Carvings of spirals and swirls decorate some of the larger stones.

An ancient well squats atop a low hill near a water stream. A strange moaning can be heard within.
