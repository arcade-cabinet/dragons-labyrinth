Caravan Camp > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex E1S14

The skeleton of a huge dragon is fossilised in one of the cliff faces here. Its wings are spread as if it was about to fly to the clouds one last time.

Several caravans are circling a large camp fire on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail. The sounds of music can be heard from afar.
