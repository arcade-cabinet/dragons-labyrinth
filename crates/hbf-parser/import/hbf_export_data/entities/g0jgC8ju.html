Description
An altar decorated with figurines of horned demons stands here and the floor is carved with claw marks. There are some big jars near the far wall.
  • The jars are trapped. Loose debris is locked above the jars, released when a switch embedded in a handle is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jars contain:
    • 217 gp in coins
    • 75 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Embroidered silk handkerchief, 2 × Silver ewer
    • Magic Items: Potion of animal friendship, Potion of resistance
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a tall pointy hat in the near corner and some carved figurines next to it.
  • Searching will uncover a small wooden box holding the key (🜥) to unlock the arched bronze door (to the E) in area 35
