Cave area #12 in Caverns of the Burning Souls

Chamber > > Caverns of the Burning Souls > Area # 12

Foreshadowing
  • When listening from outside, speaking Assassins can be faintly heard from inside this area.
Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the stalactites and stalagmites and the passages have some scorch marks on them. There’s movement inside.
  • There are 4 Assassins inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.

    Assassin

    CR: 8 (3900 XP)
    AC: 15 (studded leather armor)
    HP: (12d8 + 24)
    Speed: Walk 30 ft.
    STRDEXCONINTWISCHA
    11 +1 16 +3 14 +2 13 +2 11 +1 10
    • Saving Throws: Dex +6, Int +4
    • Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
    • Languages: Thieves' cant plus any two languages
    • Assassinate: During its first turn, the assassin has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn. Any hit the assassin scores against a TAG:status creature is a critical hit.
    • Evasion: If the assassin is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, the assassin instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.
    • Sneak Attack (1/Turn): The assassin deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the assassin that isn't incapacitated and the assassin doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
    Actions
    • Multiattack: The assassin makes two shortsword attacks.
    • Shortsword: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Inflicts 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
    • Light Crossbow: +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Inflicts 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
