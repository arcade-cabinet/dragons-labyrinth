Hex W2S48 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Prismatic Stones > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex W2S48

Ancient artwork decorates the stone here, daubed in the red clay of the nearby caves. Generations of artists have added to this work, judging by the many handprints.

Prismatic stones orbit a large spherical crystal near the base of the tallest mountain. A soft hum emanates from the mesmerising display. Anyone trying to touch or grab the crystals will lose 1d6 HP from an elecrtical jolt.
