Doorways
  • S side - secret door (requiring a passphrase)
    • If detected, a gentle voice will sing "Eternal". The full passphrase is in area 43. Sing or use the phrase in a song to open.
  • W side - passage to area 33.
Description
Countless creepy crawlies all over rush back into the darkness and there are some splatters of dark matter on the walls. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of an orc. Searching it will uncover:
    • 45 gp in coins
    • 90 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Obsidian, 2 × Lapis lazuli, Turquoise, 2 × Rhodochrosite, Azurite, Tiger eye, Banded agate
    • Magic Items: Mithral armor, Scroll2nd of Ray of Enfeeblement (Warlock)
    • Sling (Unusable)
  • There are also many candles, burnt out in a pile of wax on an altar and some torn pieces of clothing next to it.
