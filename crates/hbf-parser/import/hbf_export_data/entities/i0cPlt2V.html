Hex E4S26 in Black Shield Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Wagons > > The Lands of Vo'il > Black Shield Timberlands > Hex E4S26

Colourful flowers, berry shrubs and mushrooms grow under the woods here, dense enough to almost entirely cover a large number of human-like bones.

There are four abandoned and half-broken wagons deep in the woods.
