Foreshadowing
  • A few single silver pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the stalactites and stalagmites and the flowstones are carved with claw marks. There is a large barrel near the far wall.
  • The barrel is trapped. Loose rubble is locked above the barrel, released when a switch hidden in a rim is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the barrel contains:
    • 162 gp in coins
    • 60 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Obsidian, Lapis lazuli, 2 × Azurite, Moss agate, Banded agate
    • Magic Items: Potion of gaseous form, Potion of heroism
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a small empty bottle near the wall on the left and some small mounds of ashes next to it.
