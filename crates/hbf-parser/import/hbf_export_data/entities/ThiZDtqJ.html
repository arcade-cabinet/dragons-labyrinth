Dungeon area #6 in Crypt of the Corrupted Order

Ceremonial Chamber > > Crypt of the Corrupted Order > Area # 6

Doorways
  • E side - rectangular marble door (Barricaded)
  • W side - round bronze door (Locked 🝬)
    • The key is in area 35
  • N side - passage to area 21.
Description
A red-painted ceremonial straw-doll is hanging from above and there are some rusty stains on the hidden alcoves. There’s a large elevated oval pool on the floor.
  • The pool contains blood-like liquid. If the pool is disturbed, it will start overflowing, rapidly flooding the area. If the doors are closed, it could drown anyone here.
  • A rusted pile of chains can be found in a large basin.
  • Searching will uncover an old hollow torch holding the key (🜸) to unlock the arched bronze door (to the E) in area 32
  • There’s a crank lever that can open the secret door in area 14 (to the N)
