Doorways

Description
Tall shelves made of rotting wood are fixtured here and there are a few mossy patches on the corner sides. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a poisonous gas trap here. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if removing a specific object from its original place, causing the gas to come out of slits in the wall. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of an orc. Searching it will uncover:
    • 78 gp in coins
    • Thieves’ tools (Usable)
    • Sack (small) (Usable)
  • There’s also the rotting hide of a large animal below an arch and some broken arrows spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a crack in the floor holding the key (🜮) to unlock the arched marble door (to the W) in area 17
