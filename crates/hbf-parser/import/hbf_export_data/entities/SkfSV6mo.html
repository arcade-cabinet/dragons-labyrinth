Hex W3S32 in Grey Mist Snowlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Colossal Footprints > > The Lands of Vo'il > Grey Mist Snowlands > Hex W3S32

The ice here is thick but broken and cracked. It is a challenge to find a safe path to follow, and quite often, a leap is required to move about.

Colossal footprints mark the ground on the icy plateau. The imprints could fit an entire wagon within.
