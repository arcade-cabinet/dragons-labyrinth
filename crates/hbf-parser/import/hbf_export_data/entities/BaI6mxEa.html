Hex S33 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Iceborn Wilderness > Hex S33

This part of the jungle has a very dense undergrowth of bushes and vines. A sword or a machete is likely needed to carve the way forward.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found deep in the jungle.
