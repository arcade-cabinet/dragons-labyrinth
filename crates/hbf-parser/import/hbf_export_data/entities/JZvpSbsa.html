Dense fog covers the wet ground here. It is difficult to tell what lies ahead or what lurks under your feet.

A dizzyingly complex maze sprawls on a dry patch of land. The labyrinthine paths weaving unfathomable patterns through the puzzle. A desperate cry erupts from within, the voice begging for help with primal urgency.
