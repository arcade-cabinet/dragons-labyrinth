Colossal Footprints > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goblinchaser Jungle > Hex E5S35

Dense ivy and vegetation cover an old skeleton of a dragon, as if sleeping peacefully in a clearing surrounded by the jungle trees.

Colossal footprints mark the ground where the jungle meets a tall mountain. The imprints could fit an entire wagon within.
