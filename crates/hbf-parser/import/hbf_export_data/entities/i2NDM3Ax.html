Dungeon area #15 in Tomb of the Cursed Pits

Ceremonial Chamber > > Tomb of the Cursed Pits > Area # 15

Doorways
  • E side - arched bronze door (Stuck)
  • N side - arched wooden door (Half-broken)
  • S side - passage to area 5.
Description
Five or six old quern stones lay around here and the entryway has several spots of yellow ooze on it. There’s a large elevated oval pool on the floor.
  • The pool is filled with stinky yellowish liquid. Gazing into the pool will reflect a demonic entity that will secretly possess anyone looking at it. Save vs Spells or secretly serve the demon's evil intentions until a Bless or a similar spell is used.
  • There’s also a collection of offerings, long ago forgotten near the wall on the far end and some feathers spread all over the place.
