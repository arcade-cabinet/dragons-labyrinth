Hex E2S44 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Colossal Footprints > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex E2S44

The path here leads to a cliffside, more than a hundrad feet tall. Planks of wood attached against the rocky wall form the only way ahead.

Colossal footprints mark the ground across a seemingly bottomless chasm, where a rope bridge meets an overhang. The imprints could fit an entire wagon within.
