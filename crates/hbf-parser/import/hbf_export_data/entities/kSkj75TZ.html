Hex E3S42 in Goldenswan Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Ancient Artifact > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldenswan Timberlands > Hex E3S42

Colourful flowers, berry shrubs and mushrooms grow under the woods here, dense enough to almost entirely cover a large number of human-like bones.

When exploring this area, a giant triangular artifact made of polished iron, with large pointy spears attached, can be found wedged next to a clearing.

There's a 1-in-6 chance to find a secret door leading inside the artifact and a successful INT check will allow using a contraption inside it.

If used, the object will magically levitate and will travel 1d6 hexes in a matter of minutes before crashing into the ground again, dealing 1d6 damage to anyone inside.
