Description
An unsealed marble sarcophagus is laying here and the ceiling is covered with fractures. There is a small crate near the far wall.
  • The crate is trapped. There's a poisoned needle embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 171 gp in coins
    • 500 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): jasper, 2 × Bloodstone, 2 × Chalcedony, Chrysoprase, 2 × Star rose quartz, Moonstone, Citrine
    • Magic Items: Bag of holding, Potion of growth
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a collection of offerings, long ago forgotten near the wall on the left and some thick spiderwebs next to it.
