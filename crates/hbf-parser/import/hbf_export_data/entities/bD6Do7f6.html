Hex E5S40 in Hell's Gate Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

A Moving Contraption > > The Lands of Vo'il > Hell's Gate Desert > Hex E5S40

A giant perfect wall of stones stands lonely and unconnected in the middle of the wasteland, with only empty stretches of sand to surround it.

A great contraption rumbles beside the ruins of a pyramid. Its thunderous report shaking the ground as it travels, massive wheels carving furrows in the ground.
