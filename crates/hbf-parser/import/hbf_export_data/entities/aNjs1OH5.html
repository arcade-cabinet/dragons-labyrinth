Cave area #9 in Caverns of the Burning Souls

Chamber > > Caverns of the Burning Souls > Area # 9

Description
It is dark and damp here and the tunnel is scorched with burn marks. There’s also a colourful glow emitting from the ground.
  • There are a few dozen colourful bioluminescent mushrooms growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming these will gain the same effect as if they consumed a Potion of vitality.
  • There’s also an empty drinking flask in the near corner and some dried flowers spread all over the place.
