A huge, lush tree is standing in a clearing here, covered with colourful flowers and holding a sweet berry inside. Birds fly cheerfully around it.

A great contraption rumbles near a water stream. Its thunderous report shaking the ground as it travels, massive wheels carving furrows in the ground.
