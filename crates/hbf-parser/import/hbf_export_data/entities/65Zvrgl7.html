Dungeon area #1 in Tomb of the Unspoken Skeletons

Ceremonial Chamber > > Tomb of the Unspoken Skeletons > Area # 1

Doorways
  • S side - rectangular wooden door (Locked 🝓)
    • The key is in area 16
  • W side - rectangular wooden door (Trapped)
    • There's a concealed 10 foot deep pit next to the door. When undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if pulling a wooden lever fixed on the wall beside it. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
Description
Large symbols and letters were painted all over the place and there are some ancient carvings on the ceiling. There’s a large round pool in the middle of the floor.
  • The pool contains stinky yellowish liquid. Gazing into the pool will reflect a demonic entity that will secretly possess anyone looking at it. Save vs Spells or secretly serve the demon's evil intentions until a Bless or a similar spell is used.
  • There’s also a stagnant puddle in the far corner and some hundred tiny bones spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a nook in the wall holding the key (🝒) to unlock the arched wooden door (to the E) in area 12
  • There’s a rope-pull lever that can open the secret door in area 18 (to the N)
  • The words "Eternal Soul" in Chaotic symbols are graffitied on the ceiling. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 7 (to the E)
