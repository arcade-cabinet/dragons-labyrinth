Hex S13 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

A small patch of dark clouds circle an even darker peak. Jolts of lightning flash from within, followed by rumbling thunders.

There's a recently abandoned village on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail.
