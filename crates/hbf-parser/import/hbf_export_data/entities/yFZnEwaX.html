Description
Old and rotting food remains are stacked in several piles and the corner sides are covered with burn marks. There’s a dead body here.

    • 61 gp in coins
    • 300 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): 2 × jasper, Carnelian, Chalcedony, Chrysoprase, Citrine
    • Thieves’ tools (Usable)
  • Three large bird skulls can be found below an arch.
