Hex E3S45 in Darkfall Plains (The Lands of Vo'il)

Smoking Crater > > The Lands of Vo'il > Darkfall Plains > Hex E3S45

A huge, rocky outcrop rises from the plain beside a meandering river, the dusty stone monolith commanding views for miles around.

A smoking crater rumbles day and night in a valley. The crater is belching fire and smoke into the sky like an angry dragon. Buried inside the crater is the burnt remains of a small dragon.
