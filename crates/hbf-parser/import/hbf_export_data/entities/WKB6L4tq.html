Dungeon area #25 in Tomb of the Cursed Pits

Library > > Tomb of the Cursed Pits > Area # 25

Doorways
  • S side - round iron door (Half-broken)
  • W side - arched wooden door (Trapped)
    • There's a concealed 10 foot deep pit next to the door. When undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if pulling a wooden lever fixed on the wall beside it. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • N side - arched iron door (Locked 🝟)
    • The key is in area 3
Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the corners of the room and the entryway has some cracks on it. There’s a large circular pool embedded in the center of the floor.
  • The pool has stinky yellowish liquid inside it. Gazing into the pool will reflect a demonic entity that will secretly possess anyone looking at it. Save vs Spells or secretly serve the demon's evil intentions until a Bless or a similar spell is used.
  • There’s also a nest of straw and blankets near the wall on the far end and some small iron cages spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a goat’s skull holding the key (🜹) to unlock the rectangular bronze door (to the N) in area 12
