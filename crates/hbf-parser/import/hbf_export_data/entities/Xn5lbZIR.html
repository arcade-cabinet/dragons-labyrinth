Hex E7S39 in Goldenswan Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Pendulum > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldenswan Timberlands > Hex E7S39

The sallow trees bow and scrape, dusty leaves brushing the ancient, over-grown road below. A way stone—weathered beyond recognition—marks the distance to a long-gone settlement.

The spectral form of a towering, ghostly pendulum swings with a vicious rhythm next to a clearing. Beneath the deadly apparition the fresh corpse of Pegasus lies in two, bisected by the umbral blade.
