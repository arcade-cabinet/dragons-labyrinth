Foreshadowing
  • A successful listen roll will also reveal a faint sound of crying coming from inside this area.
Description
The flowstone on the ground shimmers through the darkness and the stalactites are covered with tufts of fungus. A person is chained to the wall and there’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a spring-loaded spears trap here, hidden inside shafts in the floor. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping on pressure plate. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the violent thrust of the jumping spears.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 59 gp in coins
    • Magic Items: Immovable rod, Potion of animal friendship, Potion of greater healing
    • Holy Symbol (Unusable)
    • Shortbow (Unusable)
    • Warm Cloak (Unusable)
  • A broken music box can be found in the near corner.
  • The chained person is Freyrith of Devilville’s nephew, Faeryn, from Palemoon .
