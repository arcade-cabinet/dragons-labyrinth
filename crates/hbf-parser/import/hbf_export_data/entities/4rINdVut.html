Dungeon area #74 in Crypt of the Mourning Goblin

Pantry > > Crypt of the Mourning Goblin > Area # 74

Doorways
  • S side - arched wooden door (Barricaded)
  • W side - passage to area 80.
Description
It is dark and damp here and there are some claw marks on the entryway. There’s a large round pool in the middle of the floor.
  • The pool has crystal clear liquid inside it. If anything dead is thrown into the pool, a demon (or any other referee elected creature) will emerge from within.
  • There’s also a blanket, laid out and covering something small near the wall on the right and some broken pieces of wood next to it.
