Description
Five or six cauldrons were placed on top of stone-made blocks and the ceiling has a few mossy patches on it. There are some small crates near the far wall.
  • The crates are trapped. Loose rubble is locked above the crates, released when a switch embedded in one of the wooden planks is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crates contain:
    • 165 gp in coins
    • 200 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Carnelian, Citrine, 2 × Quartz
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • An empty drinking flask can be found near the wall on the right.
  • Searching will uncover a goat’s skull holding the key (🜟) to unlock the round wooden door (to the W) in area 31
