Doorways

Description
The air in this area is dense and misty and there are a few claw marks on the walls. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • Lying on the floor is a dust covered human skeleton. Searching it will uncover:
    • 61 gp in coins
    • Magic Items: Dust of sneezing and choking, Sling bullets (+1)
    • Dagger (Usable)
    • Shortbow (Usable)
    • Pole (10’ long, wooden) (Unusable)
    • The key (🜊) to unlock the odd-shaped marble door (to the W) in area 28
  • There are also three large bird skulls in an alcove and some small iron cages spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a goat’s skull holding the key (🝪) to unlock the rectangular bronze door (to the W) in area 64
  • The words "Magical Goblin" in Deepcommon language are carved on one of the walls. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 50 (to the E)
