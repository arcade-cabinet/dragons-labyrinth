Foreshadowing
  • When listening from outside, speaking Drows can be faintly heard from inside this area.
Description
This area is dark and cold and the rock faces are covered with splatters of dark matter. A violent shout in an unknown language is coming from inside.
  • There are 9 Drows inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.
