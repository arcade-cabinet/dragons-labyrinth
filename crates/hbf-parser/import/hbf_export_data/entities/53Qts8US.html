Description
This area stinks from excrements and the rock faces are crumbling. There is a small chest near the far wall.
  • The chest is trapped. A strong acid ooze was applied on the chest. A successful DC18 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check will help avoid touching it; otherwise succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d4 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the chest contains:
    • 170 gp in coins
    • 500 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Sardonyx, 3 × Bloodstone, Carnelian, Chalcedony, Star rose quartz, Moonstone, 2 × Quartz
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a small empty bottle in the near corner and some seeds next to it.
