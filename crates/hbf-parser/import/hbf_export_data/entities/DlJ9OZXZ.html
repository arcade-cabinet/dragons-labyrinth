Dungeon area #29 in Crypt of the Corrupted Order

Ceremonial Chamber > > Crypt of the Corrupted Order > Area # 29

Doorways
  • E side - arched bronze door (Barricaded)
  • S side - round iron door (Barricaded)
  • N side - passage to area 35.
Description
A decorated ceremonial stone was placed here and the ground is covered with spots of yellow ooze. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of a goblin. Searching it will uncover:
    • 67 gp in coins
    • Rope (50’) (Usable)
    • Shortbow (Usable)
  • There are also many candles, burnt out in a pile of wax in an alcove and some damp leaves next to it.
  • There’s a pressure plate lever that can open the secret door in area 20 (to the W)
  • A stone-carved demon face on the wall with a large gaping mouth is a magical portal. Anyone stepping into the portal will be magically teleported into area 28.
