Description
Numerous fragments of torn paper are scattered all over the place and the floor is covered with ancient carvings. There is a big bag near the far wall.
  • The bag is trapped. There's a small gas-filled vial made of an extremely fragile glass thrown casually inside. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the bag contains:
    • 297 gp in coins
    • 100 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Star rose quartz, Quartz
    • Magic Items: Bag of holding, Keoghtom's ointment
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also an old broken mirror near the wall on the far end and some tiny mushrooms spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a nook in the wall holding the key (🝱) to unlock the rectangular iron door (to the N) in area 54
