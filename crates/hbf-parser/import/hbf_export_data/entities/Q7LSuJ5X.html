Dungeon area #91 in Crypt of the Mourning Goblin

Ceremonial Chamber > > Crypt of the Mourning Goblin > Area # 91

Doorways
  • W side - round marble door (Half-broken)
  • S side - rectangular marble door (Half-broken)
  • E side - secret door (requiring a passphrase)
    • If detected, a carving on the wall will spell "Dark". The full passphrase is in area 83. Carve and complete the phrase to open.
Description
This area stinks from excrements and the floor is covered with spots of yellow ooze. There’s a dead body here.
  • There's a crossbow trap here, hidden inside a niche in the wall. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through its trip wire. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the fast and sharp arrow.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting goblin corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 79 gp in coins
    • 60 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Lapis lazuli, Turquoise, Azurite, Tiger eye, 2 × Blue quartz
    • Pole (10’ long, wooden) (Usable)
    • Sling (Usable)
  • A pile of bones can be found near the wall on the far end.
  • Searching will uncover a cloth wrap under a stone holding the key (🜊) to unlock the round wooden door (to the E) in area 26
