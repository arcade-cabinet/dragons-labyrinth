Hex E1S8 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Sylphrena's Carriage > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex E1S8

Waterfalls of lava flow from caves in a cliff, melting pieces of rock as they fall and form a river of inferno in the deep canyon.

A runaway carriage stampedes near the base of a steep cliff. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Sylphrena Whitehelm. She has piercing black eyes, soft voice and stained clothes (Calm). In the pocket: 2 cp and 2 sp.
