Description
Countless melted candles cover the place and there are several scorch marks on the ground. A person is chained to the wall and there’s also a colourful glow emitting from the ground.
  • A few green and purple bioluminescent mushrooms are growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming them will suffer a severe stomach ache and a -2 penalty to attack rolls for 1d6 turns.
  • There’s also a tall pointy hat on an altar and some ripped pieces of old rope spread all over the place.
