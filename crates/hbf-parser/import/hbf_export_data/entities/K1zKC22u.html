Abandoned Cart > > The Lands of Vo'il > Black Shield Timberlands > Hex E4S31

Carved into the base of a titanic tree, the Citadel of Hoviir is a truly breath-taking sight. Banners of woven vines decorate the towers, and guards clad in polished emerald armour patrol the battlements.

A rickety wooden cart idles next to a clearing. Inside the cart there are several crates marked with a strange symbol, including one crate with its lid ajar. Within the crate, nestled amongst the straw packing, are several multi-coloured potions. The bottles are unlabelled.
