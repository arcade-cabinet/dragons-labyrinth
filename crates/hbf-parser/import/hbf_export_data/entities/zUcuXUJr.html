Hex S53 in Aurora Bushes (The Lands of Vo'il)

Leudo's Carriage > > The Lands of Vo'il > Aurora Bushes > Hex S53

Dozens of giant dead tree trunks are laying on the ground here, covered with moss and undergrowth. Some of them seem to have a face, with a frozen expression of agony.

A runaway carriage stampedes where the jungle meets a tall mountain. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Leudo of Balaal. He has sleepy red-looking eyes, a long red beard and lip ring (Disorganized). In the pocket: 2 cp and 2 sp.
