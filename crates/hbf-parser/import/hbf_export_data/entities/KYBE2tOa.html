Colourful plants and mushrooms grow within the undergrowth here - almost densely enough to hide the bones of an alien creature.

The well-maintained Cottage of Electo of Devilville can be found deep in the jungle.

  • Electo is seeking solitude from others. He has piercing dark eyes, a scruff and lip ring (Joyful). In the pocket: 2 cp.
    • Hearing strange noises at night and fearing someone or something will raid the Cottage.
  • There's a 2 in 6 chance (daily) of encountering Stirges (5) here.
