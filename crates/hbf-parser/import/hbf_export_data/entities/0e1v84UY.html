Foreshadowing
  • A successful listen roll will also reveal a faint sound of crying coming from inside this area.
Doorways
  • N side - rectangular wooden door (Barricaded)
  • W side - passage to area 44.
Description
This place seems to be an old dungeon cell and the corner sides have several spots of green rot on them. A person is chained to the wall and faint colourful light is emitting from everywhere.
  • A few green and purple bioluminescent mushrooms are growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming them will suffer a severe stomach ache and a -2 penalty to attack rolls for 1d6 turns.
  • Several large decorative pillows can be found near the wall on the left.
  • There’s a rope-pull lever that can open the secret door in area 55 (to the W)
  • The words "Dark Horror" in Elvish script were written down on a torn piece of parchment. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 47 (to the S)
  • The chained person is Galadrielle Theodefrid’s husband, Volkward, from Palemoon .
