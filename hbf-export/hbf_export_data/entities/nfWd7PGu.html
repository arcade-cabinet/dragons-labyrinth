Foreshadowing
  • A successful listen roll will also reveal a faint sound of crying coming from inside this area.
Doorways
  • N side - secret door (requiring a passphrase)
    • If detected, one of the characters will uncontrollably gesture or act the word "Dagger". The full passphrase is in area 35. Gesture or mime the complete passphrase to open.
  • W side - passage to area 14.
Description
An unsealed wooden sarcophagus is laying here and the ground is heavily weathered. A person is chained to the wall and there’s a large elevated oval pool on the floor.
  • The pool has dark, tar-colored liquid inside it. The liquid is a strong acid. When it comes in touch with human skin, it will inflict 1d4 of damage each round until it is completely washed off.
  • There’s also a small empty bottle in a large basin and some damp leaves next to it.
  • The chained person is Thalassarionus of Dorith’s nephew, Wigher, from Devilville .
