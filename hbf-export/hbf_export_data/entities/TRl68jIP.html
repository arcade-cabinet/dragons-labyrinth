Foreshadowing
  • A few single silver pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
The cave earth here makes the ground feel sticky and there are several fractures on the cavern walls. There are some small crates near the far wall.
  • The crates are trapped. There's a small gas-filled vial made of an extremely fragile glass embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crates contain:
    • 179 gp in coins
    • 500 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): 2 × jasper, 2 × Bloodstone, Carnelian, 2 × Star rose quartz, Citrine, 2 × Quartz
    • Magic Items: 2 × Potion of vitality, Scroll6th of Guards and Wards (Wizard)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a clear puddle in a large basin and some dry remains of food spread all over the place.
