Hex E4S25 in Javelin Wetlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Javelin Wetlands > Hex E4S25

Trees rise on stilt-like roots from the stinking water, as if lifting themselves above the grime and muck of the swamp. Hives of buzzing insects hang from several branches.

There's a crowded fighting pit beside a stream of water. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

2,640 gp will be awarded to the winner.
