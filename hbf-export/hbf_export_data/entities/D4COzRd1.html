Dungeon area #68 in Crypt of the Mourning Goblin

Doorways
  • N side - rectangular wooden door (Half-broken)
  • W side - arched iron door (Half-broken)
  • S side - rectangular wooden door (Barricaded)
  • E side - passage to area 45.
Description
Various torturing devices are fixtured here and there are several glimmers of mineral veins on the entryway. There’s a dead body here.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting orc corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 68 gp in coins
    • 150 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, Cloth-of-gold vestments, 2 × Copper chalice with silver filigree, Silver ewer, Small mirror set in a painted wooden frame
    • Magic Items: Potion of clairvoyance, Potion of fire giant strength, Potion of frost giant strength, Potion of mind reading
    • Sack (small) (Usable)
    • Leather Hat (Usable)
    • Sack (small) (Unusable)
  • There’s also a pile of bones in the far corner and some feathers spread all over the place.
  • There’s a pressure plate lever that can open the secret door in area 62 (to the S)
  • The words "Fearful Heretic" in Gargoyle language are carved on one of the walls. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 15 (to the E)
