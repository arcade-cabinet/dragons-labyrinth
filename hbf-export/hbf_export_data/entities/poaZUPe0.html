Foreshadowing
  • A few single silver pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
Oversized fossilised mushrooms are covering this area and there are a few ancient carvings on the flowstones. There are some big chests near the far wall.
  • The chests are trapped. Loose debris is locked above the chests, released when a switch hidden under a pulling ring is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the chests contain:
    • 76 gp in coins
    • Magic Items: Chime of opening, Potion of gaseous form, Potion of superior healing
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also an old, ripped backpack near the wall on the far end and some garlic leftovers spread all over the place.
