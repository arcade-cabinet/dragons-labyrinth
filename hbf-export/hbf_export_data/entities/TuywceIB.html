Hex S32 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Bones litter the muddy sprawl before a gaping cavern, the entrance partially obscured by swollen, thorny vines. The stench of death saturates the air.

The headless rotting remains of a beheaded Guardian Naga can be found where the jungle meets a tall mountain.
