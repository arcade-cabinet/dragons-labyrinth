Hex W1S36 in Grey Mist Snowlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Distress in a Maze > > The Lands of Vo'il > Grey Mist Snowlands > Hex W1S36

The snow here is soft to walk on. Too soft. In some places, taking the next step means falling down deep into a sinkhole.

A dizzyingly complex maze sprawls between two snowy giant boulders. The labyrinthine paths weaving unfathomable patterns through the puzzle. A desperate cry erupts from within, the voice begging for help with primal urgency.
