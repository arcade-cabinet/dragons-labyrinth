Birdsong graces this sun-dappled clearing, scoring the majesty of nature’s unblemished tranquillity with a delightful symphony. A clear pool rests at its heart, providing nourishment for a stooped fawn.

There’s a stone-made signaling tower deep in the woods. Finding the tower will eliminate any risk of getting lost in this region.
