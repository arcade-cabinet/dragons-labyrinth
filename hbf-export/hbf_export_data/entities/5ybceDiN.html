Description
This area is dark and cold and the ceiling is covered with scorch marks. Faint colourful light is emitting from everywhere.
  • There are a few colourful bioluminescent mushrooms growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming these will be paralyzed with mind twisting hallucinations for 1d6 turns.
  • A pile of bones can be found below an arch.
  • Searching will uncover a small wooden box holding the key (🜙) to unlock the round wooden door (to the W) in area 6
