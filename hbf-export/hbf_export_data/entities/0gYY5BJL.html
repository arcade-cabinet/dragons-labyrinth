Description
This place was likely used to store food in the past and the ground is covered with spots of yellow ooze. There are some very big crates near the far wall.
  • The crates are trapped. There's a poisoned needle embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crates contain:
    • 152 gp in coins
    • 250 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): jasper, Chalcedony, Star rose quartz, 2 × Moonstone
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • A rusted pile of chains can be found in the far corner.
  • There’s a crank lever that can open the secret door in area 37 (to the W)
