Description
Various embalming tools are hanging beside strips of linen and the hidden alcoves have several fractures on them. There are some large barrels near the far wall.
  • The barrels are trapped. Loose stones are locked above the barrels, released when a switch hidden in a rim is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the barrels contain:
    • 189 gp in coins
    • 100 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): 2 × Obsidian, Hematite, Lapis lazuli, Turquoise, Azurite, Eye agate, 2 × Banded agate, Blue quartz
    • Magic Items: Potion of heroism, Potion of invulnerability, Potion of stone giant strength, Potion of superior healing
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
