Description
Four or five cauldrons were placed on top of stone-made blocks and the walls have several claw marks on them. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • There's a crossbow trap here, hidden inside a niche in the wall. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if removing a specific object from its original place. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the fast and sharp arrow.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of an orc. Searching it will uncover:
    • 46 gp in coins
  • Many candles, burnt out in a pile of wax can be found in an alcove.
