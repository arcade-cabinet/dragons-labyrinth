Hex E6S48 in Nightmare Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Nightmare Desert > Hex E6S48

A giant perfect wall of stones stands lonely and unconnected in the middle of the wasteland, with only empty stretches of sand to surround it.

An improvised but crowded fighting arena stands near an ancient monument. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

1,048 gp will be awarded to the winner.
