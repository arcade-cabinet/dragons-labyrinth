Description
Countless creepy crawlies all over rush back into the darkness and there are several mossy patches on the entryway. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a crushing swinging boulder trap here, hidden inside the wall. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception) check, it will trigger if causing a sound sensitive membrane to vibrate. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 36 gp in coins
  • There’s also a collection of offerings, long ago forgotten on an altar and some small stones with runes etchings next to it.
