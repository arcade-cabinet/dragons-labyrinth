Description
Four or five old quern stones lay around here and the walls are scorched with burn marks. There are some large bags near the far wall.
  • The bags are trapped. A strong acid ooze was applied on the bags. A successful DC18 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check will help avoid touching it; otherwise succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d4 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the bags contain:
    • 175 gp in coins
    • 400 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): 2 × Sardonyx, jasper, Bloodstone, 2 × Carnelian, Star rose quartz, Zircon
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a broken wooden box near the wall on the right and some shreded pages of a book next to it.
  • Searching will uncover a goat’s skull holding the key (🝬) to unlock the round bronze door (to the W) in area 6
