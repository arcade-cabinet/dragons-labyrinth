Hex W2S11 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Wagons > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex W2S11

Small unexplained earthquakes tremble the rocks here a few times a day. Loose rocks and boulders may block the pathways, or even worse, fall from above unexpectedly.

There are three abandoned and half-broken wagons across a seemingly bottomless chasm, where a rope bridge meets an overhang.
