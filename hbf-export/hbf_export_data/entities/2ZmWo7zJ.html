Hex W2S50 in Aurora Bushes (The Lands of Vo'il)

Sacrificial Grounds > > The Lands of Vo'il > Aurora Bushes > Hex W2S50

The trees here are big and tall. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

There's a sacrificial site with burnt human remains deep in the jungle.
