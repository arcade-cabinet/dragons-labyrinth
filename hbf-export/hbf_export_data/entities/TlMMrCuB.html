Description
The air in this area is dense and misty and there are a few patches of dampness on the ground. There’s something moving inside.
  • There are 2 Chuuls inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.

    Chuul

    CR: 4 (1100 XP)
    AC: 16 (natural armor)
    HP: (11d10 + 33)
    Speed: Walk 30 ft. Swim 30 ft.
    STRDEXCONINTWISCHA
    19 +5 10 16 +3 5 -3 11 +1 5 -3
    • Immunities: poison
    • Senses: darkvision 60 ft.
    • Alignment: Chaotic Evil
    • Languages: understands Deep Speech but can't speak
    • Amphibious: The chuul can breathe air and water.
    • Sense Magic: The chuul senses magic within 120 feet of it at will. This trait otherwise works like the TAG:spell spell but isn't itself magical.
    Actions
    • Multiattack: The chuul makes two pincer attacks. If the chuul is grappling a creature, the chuul can also use its tentacles once.
    • Pincer: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Inflicts 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage. The target is grappled (escape DC14) if it is a Large or smaller creature and the chuul doesn't have two other creatures grappled.
    • Tentacles: One creature grappled by the chuul must succeed on a DC13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. Until this poison ends, the target is paralyzed. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
