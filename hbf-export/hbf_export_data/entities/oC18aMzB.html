Hex E1S4 in Vicious Crags (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Vicious Crags > Hex E1S4

The narrow and winding trails here are on the faces of the cliffs. Carelessness or combat are especially risky here.

There's a crowded fighting pit across a seemingly bottomless chasm, where a rope bridge meets an overhang. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

774 gp will be awarded to the winner.
