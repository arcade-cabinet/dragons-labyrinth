Hex E2S33 in Hell's Gate Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

Ellidyr's Carriage > > The Lands of Vo'il > Hell's Gate Desert > Hex E2S33

A plateau of sand-blasted rock provides a welcome respite from the burning desert sands. The stone is smooth and cool.

A runaway carriage stampedes near an ancient monument. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Ellidyr of Dokar. He has glowing black eyes, a goatee beard and a missing tooth (Apprehensive). In the pocket: 2 cp, 2 sp and a vial with a dark liquid.
