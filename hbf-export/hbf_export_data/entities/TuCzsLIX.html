Foreshadowing
  • A few single silver pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
Oversized fossilised mushrooms are covering this area and the rock faces have several ancient carvings on them. There is a big crate near the far wall.
  • The crate is trapped. There's a poisoned needle embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 211 gp in coins
    • 100 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): jasper, Quartz
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • Knitting needles and yarn can be found near the wall on the far end.
  • A wooden barrel contains Wigher Cadhoiarn’s from Dokar custom dagger (actually a Dagger +3 )
