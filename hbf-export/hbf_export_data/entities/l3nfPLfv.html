Hex S28 in Grey Mist Snowlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Leandra's Carriage > > The Lands of Vo'il > Grey Mist Snowlands > Hex S28

A frozen waterfall is the only way to march south-west from here and will require climbing or rappelling to cross it.

A runaway carriage stampedes near a hot spring. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Leandra Natalisma. She has short and wavy white hair, haunted blue eyes and a double chin (Unsatisfied). In the pocket: a dirty handkerchief, a smoking pipe and 2 gp.
