Doorways
  • N side - rectangular wooden door (Locked 🜪)
    • The key is in area 1
  • E side - arched bronze door (Trapped)
    • There's a concealed 10 foot deep pit next to the door. When undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if pulling a wooden lever fixed on the wall beside it. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • S side - secret door (concealed)
    • This doorway is concealed as part of a fresco on the wall
  • W side - passage to area 38.
Description
Countless creepy crawlies all over rush back into the darkness and the ground is covered with spots of green rot. There’s a dead body here.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 37 gp in coins
    • Holy Symbol (Unusable)
    • Hammer (small) (Usable)
  • A smashed lyre can be found in a large basin.
  • A plain looking wooden chest contains Feyrindor of Devilville’s from Ashamar glass ball (actually a Crystal Ball )
