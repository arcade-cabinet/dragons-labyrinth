Hex W6S43 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Wagons > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex W6S43

Dense fog makes travelling on the trails here difficult and slow. The reduced visibility renders it almost impossible to scout for danger.

Three heavily damaged wagons were left to decay near the base of the tallest mountain.
