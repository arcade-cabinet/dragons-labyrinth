Hex S39 in Bonecrusher Plains (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Pendulum > > The Lands of Vo'il > Bonecrusher Plains > Hex S39

A vast field of colourful flowers covers the shallow hills here. Butterflies and bees hop from flower to flower.

The spectral form of a towering, ghostly pendulum swings with a vicious rhythm between two hills. Beneath the deadly apparition the fresh corpse of a Wereboar lies in two, bisected by the umbral blade.
