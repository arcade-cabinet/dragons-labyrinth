Hex E3S37 in Bonecrusher Plains (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Bonecrusher Plains > Hex E3S37

A tall stone tower fell and now lay crushed on the ground. A few small bunnies run and hide under its stone bricks.

There's a crowded fighting pit at the top of a hill. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules

  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

2,400 gp will be awarded to the winner.
