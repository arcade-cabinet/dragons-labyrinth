Description
This area stinks from excrements and the corner sides are covered with ancient carvings. There is a small crate near the far wall.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 148 gp in coins
    • 50 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, Cloth-of-gold vestments
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a rusty short sword near the wall on the right and some small iron cages next to it.
