Description
This place seems to have been used for torturing and the entryway has a few patches of dampness on it. There is a big bag near the far wall.
  • The bag is trapped. There's a small gas-filled blister made of lizard's intestine thrown casually inside. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the bag contains:
    • 229 gp in coins
    • 60 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Hematite, 2 × Turquoise, 3 × Blue quartz
    • Magic Items: Horseshoes of speed, Potion of heroism
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a boot, skeletal foot still inside near the wall on the left and some brightly colored leaves spread all over the place.
