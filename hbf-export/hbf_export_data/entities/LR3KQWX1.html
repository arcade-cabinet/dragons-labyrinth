Hex S8 in Vicious Crags (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Vicious Crags > Hex S8

Dense fog makes travelling on the trails here difficult and slow. The reduced visibility renders it almost impossible to scout for danger.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found high in the mountains.
