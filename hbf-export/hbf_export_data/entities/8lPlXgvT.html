Hex E2S1 in Vicious Crags (The Lands of Vo'il)

Abandoned Village > > The Lands of Vo'il > Vicious Crags > Hex E2S1

The remains of a ruined fort guard the mountain pass, the frost-rimed wooden palisades and towers cast down decades ago, much like the society who built them.

The burned remains of a small village can be found on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail.
