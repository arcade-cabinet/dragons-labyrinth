Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the corners of the room and the ceiling is covered with rusty stains. You feel the warm stench of a monster’s breath.
  • There are 4 Phase Spiders inside, ripping an old torn sack of rotting potatoes. They will attack anyone stepping in.
