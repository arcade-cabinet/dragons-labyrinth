Description

Trap!
There's an acidic vapour trap here. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through its trip wire, causing a spray of acid to come out of a hole in the wall. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d6 damage by acid.
