Hex S26 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Iceborn Wilderness > Hex S26

The trees here are big and tall. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found next to a clearing.
