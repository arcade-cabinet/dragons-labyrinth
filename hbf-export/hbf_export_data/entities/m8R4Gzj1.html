Hex W2S13 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Whirlpool > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex W2S13

Giant boulders are hanging in mid air by some kind of invisible magic. Some have rope ladders attached, reaching all the way down for anyone to climb.

A spectral whirlpool of ghostly faces can be seen high in the mountains. The trapped souls howling in silent agony.
