Foreshadowing
  • A successful listening roll could detect the sounds that the Salamanders are making inside this area.
Description
You hear the sound of water dripping down and the flowstones are carved with claw marks. Looking down, you see countless fragments of small animal skeletons.
  • There are 3 Salamanders inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.
