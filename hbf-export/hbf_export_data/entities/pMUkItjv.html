Eirwen's Curiosities (Exotic Goods)

Exotica curator: Eirwen of Dorith. She has piercing dark eyes, heavy makeup and big round spectacles (Remorseful). In the pocket: a deck of cards, 3 sp and a ransom note.

  • Eirwen has a grudge against Thorneval Membresia.
  • Twice a season, Eirwen will want to acquire exotic artifacts in Javelin Wetlands (hex E4S25) and will look for someone to protect her throughout the journey.
