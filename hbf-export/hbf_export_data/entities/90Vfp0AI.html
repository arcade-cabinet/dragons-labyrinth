Cave area #28 in Bowel of the Raging Pits

Cave Room > > Bowel of the Raging Pits > Area # 28

Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the stalactites and stalagmites and the tunnel is scorched with burn marks. Faint colourful light is emitting from everywhere.
  • Countless green and purple bioluminescent mushrooms are growing from cracks in the ground. Anyone consuming them will suffer a severe stomach ache and a -2 penalty to attack rolls for 1d6 turns.
  • A burnt torch can be found near the wall on the right.
