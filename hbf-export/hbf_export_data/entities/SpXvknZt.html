Hex E1S43 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Troll Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex E1S43

The skeleton of a huge dragon is fossilised in one of the cliff faces here. Its wings are spread as if it was about to fly to the clouds one last time.

The chopped head of a Troll was left to rot near the base of the tallest mountain.
