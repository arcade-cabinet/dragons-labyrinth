Hex E4S38 in Hell's Gate Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Hell's Gate Desert > Hex E4S38

An oasis shimmers on the horizon, the swaying palm fronds beckoning you towards a welcome rest in the shade beside a nourishing pool.

There's a crowded fighting pit between two giant dunes. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

2,530 gp will be awarded to the winner.
