Description
An oversized wooden sarcophagus is laying open here and there are several mossy patches on the hidden alcoves. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • There's a spring-loaded spears trap here, hidden inside shafts in the floor. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if removing a specific object from its original place. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the violent thrust of the jumping spears.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting human corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 24 gp in coins
    • 110 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Obsidian, Lapis lazuli, 2 × Turquoise, Azurite, 2 × Eye agate, Moss agate, Banded agate, 2 × Blue quartz
    • Magic Items: Keoghtom's ointment, Potion of growth, Robe of useful items
    • Shortsword (Usable)
    • Garlic (Unusable)
  • Marbles litter the area.
