Hex W2S53 in Goblinchaser Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Smoking Crater > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goblinchaser Wilderness > Hex W2S53

Moss covered ruins of an ancient stone structure emerge from within the vegetation. Carvings of spirals and swirls decorate some of the larger stones.

A smoking crater rumbles day and night where the jungle meets a tall mountain. The crater is belching fire and smoke into the sky like an angry dragon. Dug inside the crater is a small autonomous-flying metallic sphere.
