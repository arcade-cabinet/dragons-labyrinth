Hex W3S29 in Thunderwave Woodlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Theodora's Carriage > > The Lands of Vo'il > Thunderwave Woodlands > Hex W3S29

Dense ivy and vegetation cover an old skeleton of a dragon, as if sleeping peacefully in a clearing surrounded by the trees.

A runaway carriage stampedes near a water stream. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Theodora of Headbone. She has ample hips, piercing green eyes and gap teeth (Desperate).
