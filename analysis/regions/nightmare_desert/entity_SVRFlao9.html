You can clearly see bones revealing under the dunes when the wind shifts the sand. This was no land of peace.

A field of carved boulders stretches near an ancient monument. like an abandoned sculpture garden. Each rock has been carved into a face, the features exaggerated and distorted like a disturbing reflection.

A Stone Golem wanders the boulder field, ambushing those who come to investigate.
