So big and tall are the trees here. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

The impressive Castle of Landward Bellavita can be found where the woods meet a large hill.

  • Landward, a level 4 Fighter, has acquired the lands surrounding the castle and is developing it as his barony. He has thick bushy eyebrows, wild green eyes and oversized suspenders (Persuasive). In the pocket: dice and a smoking pipe.
