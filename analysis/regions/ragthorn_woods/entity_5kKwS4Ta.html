Hex E2S11 in Ragthorn Woods (The Lands of Vo'il)

Cluster of Eggs > > The Lands of Vo'il > Ragthorn Woods > Hex E2S11

Ropes tangle down from the branches here. On some of them, human-sized straw-made figures are hanging by their canvas covered heads.

A cluster of strange eggs have been hidden where the woods meet a large hill. The marbled patterns are mesmerising to behold.
