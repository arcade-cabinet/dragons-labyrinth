Hex E1S55 in Heartseeker Moors (The Lands of Vo'il)

Watchtower > > The Lands of Vo'il > Heartseeker Moors > Hex E1S55

These marshes are dark and thick. Rotting tree trunks are scattered around, some are floating, some are splintered in the black mud.

The ruins of an ancient watchtower are on the muddy lands.
