Hex E1S53 in Heartseeker Moors (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Heartseeker Moors > Hex E1S53

The trees here are dark and dripping, leaning over the murky water. There are hissing noises all around, and the air is thick and humid.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found on the muddy lands.
