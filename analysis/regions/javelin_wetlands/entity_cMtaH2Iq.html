Hex E4S24 in Javelin Wetlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Ogre Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Javelin Wetlands > Hex E4S24

These marshes are dark and thick. Rotting tree trunks are scattered around, some are floating, some are splintered in the black mud.

The headless rotting remains of a beheaded Ogre can be found on the muddy lands.
