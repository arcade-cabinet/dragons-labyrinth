Hex E8S47 in Goldenswan Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Pendulum > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldenswan Timberlands > Hex E8S47

Giant red, purple and blue mushrooms grow beside the trees here, their colors are vibrant and bright, much like gemstones.

The spectral form of a towering, ghostly pendulum swings with a vicious rhythm near a water stream. Beneath the deadly apparition the fresh corpse of a Green Hag lies in two, bisected by the umbral blade.
