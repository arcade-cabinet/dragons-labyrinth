Hex S52 in Aurora Bushes (The Lands of Vo'il)

Abandoned Village > > The Lands of Vo'il > Aurora Bushes > Hex S52

The forest is dense and dark here, but countless clusters of phosphorescent fungi are growing near the base of the trunks, creating a luminous colourful glow above the ground.

The burned remains of a small village can be found near a water stream.
