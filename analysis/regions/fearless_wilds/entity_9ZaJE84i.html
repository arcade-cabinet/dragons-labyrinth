Hex S3 in Fearless Wilds (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Fearless Wilds > Hex S3

Colourful plants and mushrooms grow within the undergrowth here - almost densely enough to hide the bones of an alien creature.

An improvised but crowded fighting arena stands near a water stream. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

1,548 gp will be awarded to the winner.
