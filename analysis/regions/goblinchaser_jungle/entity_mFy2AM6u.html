Hex E2S27 in Goblinchaser Jungle (The Lands of Vo'il)

Wagons > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goblinchaser Jungle > Hex E2S27

The trees here are big and tall. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

There are six abandoned and half-broken wagons near a water stream.
