An oversized statue of a warrior holding a sword against the ground stands proudly in a clearing here. Fresh flowers were recently placed near its base.

The spectral form of a towering, ghostly pendulum swings with a vicious rhythm next to a clearing. Beneath the deadly apparition the fresh corpse of a Will-o'-Wisp lies in two, bisected by the umbral blade.
