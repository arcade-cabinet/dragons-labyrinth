Either wind or some other force creates dozens of giant sand vortexes here, as tall as the eye can see.

The magically-hidden Observatory of Rubus of Kothian is located beside the ruins of a pyramid.

  • Rubus, a level 11 Wizard, is trying to unlock the secrets of the universe. He has bulging dark eyes, thin greying hair and long crimson robe (Lifeless). In the pocket: a comb and a smoking pipe.
    • Rubus is hoping to research Blood Crystal Shards, thought to be found in Vicious Crags. Searching for the Blood Crystal Shards will increase the chance for an encounter in that hex.
      • Finders reward is Potion of speed
