Hex E3S35 in Hell's Gate Desert (The Lands of Vo'il)

Altar > > The Lands of Vo'il > Hell's Gate Desert > Hex E3S35

A large pit throws out intolerable heat from the flames leaping from its depths, and the sand around it is glassy and brittle.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found on the dunes sands.
