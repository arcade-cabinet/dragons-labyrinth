Vast columns of red stone emerge from the dunes like the fingers of a titanic hand reaching for the sky.

A runaway carriage stampedes near an ancient monument. At the reigns, desperately trying to slow the carriage down, is Lirael of Balaal. He has thick bushy eyebrows, glowing black eyes and a limp (Unimpressed). In the pocket: a quill, an empty vial and a key.
