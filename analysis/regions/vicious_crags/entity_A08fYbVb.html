Hex W2S5 in Vicious Crags (The Lands of Vo'il)

Smoking Crater > > The Lands of Vo'il > Vicious Crags > Hex W2S5

Giant thin pillars of rocks are barely supporting even bigger plates of stone. Some of these huge mushroom-like formations have already collapsed. The others are likely to fall as well soon.

A smoking crater rumbles day and night high in the mountains. The crater is belching fire and smoke into the sky like an angry dragon. Wedged inside the crater is an elliptic and metallic tube.
