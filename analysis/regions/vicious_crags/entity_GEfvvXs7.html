Coniferous trees shroud the trail, snowy boughs creaking beneath the weight of their wintery burden. A single post marks a fork in the ancient road, the signage weathered beyond legibility.

The crumbling ruins of an ancient altar can be found across a seemingly bottomless chasm, where a rope bridge meets an overhang.
