Hex W4S38 in Moonwatcher Wetlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Pendulum > > The Lands of Vo'il > Moonwatcher Wetlands > Hex W4S38

Dense fog covers the wet ground here. It is difficult to tell what lies ahead or what lurks under your feet.

The spectral form of a towering, ghostly pendulum swings with a vicious rhythm beside a stream of water. Beneath the deadly apparition the fresh corpse of a Will-o'-Wisp lies in two, bisected by the umbral blade.
