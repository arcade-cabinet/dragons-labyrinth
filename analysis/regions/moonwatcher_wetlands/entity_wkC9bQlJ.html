Hex W2S40 in Moonwatcher Wetlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Adult Black Dragon Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Moonwatcher Wetlands > Hex W2S40

Murky dark water covers most of the land here. Rotting tree trunks, partially submerged, provide the occasional improvised beam bridge for travel.

The brutalized remains of an Adult Black Dragon were left to rot on the muddy lands.
