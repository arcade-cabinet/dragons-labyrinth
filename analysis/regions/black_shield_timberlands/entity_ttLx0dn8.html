Loamy soil clings to your boots, hungrily grasping with every step as you traverse the tangled undergrowth, and fog hangs thick in the air like a widow’s veil.

There’s a stone-made signaling tower where the woods meet a large hill. Finding the tower will eliminate any risk of getting lost in this region.
