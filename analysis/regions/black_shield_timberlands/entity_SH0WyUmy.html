Hex E5S30 in Black Shield Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Couatl Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Black Shield Timberlands > Hex E5S30

So big and tall are the trees here. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

The headless rotting remains of a beheaded Couatl can be found where the woods meet a large hill.
