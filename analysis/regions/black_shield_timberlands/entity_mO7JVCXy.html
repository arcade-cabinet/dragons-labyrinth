Hex E7S29 in Black Shield Timberlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Werewolf Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Black Shield Timberlands > Hex E7S29

So big and tall are the trees here. Their tops are almost hidden by the dense mist. Their trunks are as wide as a hut. Some of them have planks of wood attached, forming a ladder.

The chopped head of a Werewolf was left to rot near a water stream.
