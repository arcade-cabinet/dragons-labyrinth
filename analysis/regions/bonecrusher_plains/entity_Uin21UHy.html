Hex S40 in Bonecrusher Plains (The Lands of Vo'il)

Ancient Artifact > > The Lands of Vo'il > Bonecrusher Plains > Hex S40

A tall stone tower fell and now lay crushed on the ground. A few small bunnies run and hide under its stone bricks.

When exploring this area, a giant triangular artifact made of polished iron, with large pointy spears attached, can be found wedged in a valley.

There's a 1-in-6 chance to find a secret door leading inside the artifact and a successful INT check will allow using a contraption inside it.

If used, the object will magically levitate and will travel 1d6 hexes in a matter of minutes before crashing into the ground again, dealing 1d6 damage to anyone inside.
