Hex E1S59 in Fallen Star Steppe (The Lands of Vo'il)

Abandoned Cart > > The Lands of Vo'il > Fallen Star Steppe > Hex E1S59

A tall stone tower fell and now lay crushed on the ground. A few small bunnies run and hide under its stone bricks.

A rickety wooden cart idles at the top of a hill. Inside the cart there are several crates marked with a strange symbol, including one crate with its lid ajar. Within the crate, nestled amongst the straw packing, are several multi-coloured potions. The bottles are unlabelled.
