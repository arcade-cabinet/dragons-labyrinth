Hex S16 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Spectral Whirlpool > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex S16

The remains of a ruined fort guard the mountain pass, the frost-rimed wooden palisades and towers cast down decades ago, much like the society who built them.

A spectral whirlpool of ghostly faces can be seen on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail. The trapped souls howling in silent agony.
