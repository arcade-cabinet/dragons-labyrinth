Abandoned Village > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex W2S15

Waterfalls of lava flow from caves in a cliff, melting pieces of rock as they fall and form a river of inferno in the deep canyon.

The burned remains of a small village can be found near the base of the tallest mountain.
