Hex E1S12 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Fighting Arena > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex E1S12

Ancient artwork decorates the stone here, daubed in the red clay of the nearby caves. Generations of artists have added to this work, judging by the many handprints.

There's a crowded fighting pit near the base of a steep cliff. A melee fighting contest takes place here and there's a trophy for the winner.

Rules
  • One vs one only
  • No magic of any kind is allowed
  • Lawful can only fight Chaotic
  • Neutral can fight Neutral and Chaotic
  • Chaotic can fight anyone

Trophy

741 gp will be awarded to the winner.
