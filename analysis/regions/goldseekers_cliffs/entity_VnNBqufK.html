Hex W2S17 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Abandoned Village > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex W2S17

Waterfalls of lava flow from caves in a cliff, melting pieces of rock as they fall and form a river of inferno in the deep canyon.

The burned remains of a small village can be found on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail.
