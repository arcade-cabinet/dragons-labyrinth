Hex W1S12 in Goldseeker's Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Watchtower > > The Lands of Vo'il > Goldseeker's Cliffs > Hex W1S12

Small unexplained earthquakes tremble the rocks here a few times a day. Loose rocks and boulders may block the pathways, or even worse, fall from above unexpectedly.

There's a recently built but seemingly unstaffed watchtower on the edge of the cliffs, at the end of a winding steep trail.
