Hex E2S42 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Wagons > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex E2S42

A winding trail leads down through the rocky crags beneath towering, snow-capped peaks. The mountains seem to press down upon you as you travel, adding to the claustrophobic feeling the narrow path evokes.

There are six abandoned and half-broken wagons across a seemingly bottomless chasm, where a rope bridge meets an overhang.
