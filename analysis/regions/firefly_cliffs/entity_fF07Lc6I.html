Hex E1S44 in Firefly Cliffs (The Lands of Vo'il)

Trapped Bard > > The Lands of Vo'il > Firefly Cliffs > Hex E1S44

A goat skull sits upon a large, flat rock at the peak of the mountain, its hollow eyes gazing out across the valley below.

Music drifts near the base of the tallest mountain. A warm, honeyed melody inviting you to listen a while. Following the music to its source you find Hrotho, a terrified bard surrounded by sleeping monsters (from the random encounters table). He stares at you in desperation, trapped by his own lullaby and unable to escape.

Hrotho of Balaal. He has bulging red eyes, short white hair and freckles (Energetic). In the pocket: a dirty handkerchief, a smoking pipe and a death threat note.
