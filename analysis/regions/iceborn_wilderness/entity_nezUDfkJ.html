Hex S34 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Droplets of water fall down from the oversized leaves of the tall trees here. Ancient stone made totem-like pillars emerge from the undergrowth, each with a different carving of an animal.

An ancient well squats atop a low hill where the jungle meets a tall mountain. A strange moaning can be heard within.
