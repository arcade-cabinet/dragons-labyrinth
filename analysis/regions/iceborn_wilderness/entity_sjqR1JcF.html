Hex E1S33 in Iceborn Wilderness (The Lands of Vo'il)

Watchtower > > The Lands of Vo'il > Iceborn Wilderness > Hex E1S33

This part of the jungle has a very dense undergrowth of bushes and vines. A sword or a machete is likely needed to carve the way forward.

The ruins of an ancient watchtower are deep in the jungle.
