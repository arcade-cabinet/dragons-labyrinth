Hex W2S31 in Grey Mist Snowlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Dead Mammoth Remains > > The Lands of Vo'il > Grey Mist Snowlands > Hex W2S31

Gusts of wind swirl the soft snow in the air, making it difficult to walk safely and comfortably as it obscures the vision and hits any exposed skin with a freezing sting.

The chopped head of a Mammoth was left to rot on the icy plateau.
