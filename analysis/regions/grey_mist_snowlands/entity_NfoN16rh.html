Hex S29 in Grey Mist Snowlands (The Lands of Vo'il)

Trapped Bard > > The Lands of Vo'il > Grey Mist Snowlands > Hex S29

A frozen waterfall is the only way to march south-east from here and will require climbing or rappelling to cross it.

Music drifts between two snowy giant boulders. A warm, honeyed melody inviting you to listen a while. Following the music to its source you find Arntrude, a terrified bard surrounded by sleeping monsters (from the random encounters table). She stares at you in desperation, trapped by her own lullaby and unable to escape.

Arntrude of Ashamar. She has soft voice, wild green eyes and a double chin (Guilty). In the pocket: a dirty handkerchief.
