Dungeon area #7 in Tomb of the Grey Ogre

Pantry > > Tomb of the Grey Ogre > Area # 7

Doorways
  • E side - rectangular marble door (Stuck)
  • S side - passage to area 20.
Description
The air in this area is dense and misty and there are some rusty stains on the ground. There’s a large elevated oval pool on the floor.
  • The pool is filled with stinky yellowish liquid. When anything is submerged into the pool, the liquid inside will solidify around it into stone. It will take either magic or incredible external effort to break out.
  • There’s also an eyeball in a sealed jar in the far corner and some dried flowers next to it.
  • The words "Blessed Bones" in Chaotic symbols were inscribed on wooden plaque fixed to a wall. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 9 (to the W)
