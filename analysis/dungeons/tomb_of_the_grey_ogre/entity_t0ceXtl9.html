Description
Five or six half-broken sarcophagi are laying here and the walls are covered with burn marks. There is a very big crate near the far wall.
  • The crate is trapped. Loose stones are locked above the crate, released when a switch embedded in one of the wooden planks is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 177 gp in coins
    • 200 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, 2 × Carved bone statuette, Cloth-of-gold vestments, Pair of engraved bone dice, 2 × Silver ewer, Small gold bracelet
    • Magic Items: Cloak of protection, Rapier (+1), Ring of water walking
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a pile of papers in a large basin and some shreded pages of a book spread all over the place.
