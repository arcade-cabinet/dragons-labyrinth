If the target is a creature other than an undead or a construct, it must succeed on a DC12 Constitution saving throw or lose 5 (1d10) hit points at the start of each of its turns due to an infernal wound. Each time the devil hits the wounded target with this attack, the damage dealt by the wound increases by 5 (1d10). Any creature can take an action to stanch the wound with a successful DC12 Wisdom (Medicine) check. The wound also closes if the target receives magical healing.
