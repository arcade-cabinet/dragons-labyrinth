Cave area #6 in Bowel of the Raging Pits

Cave Room > > Bowel of the Raging Pits > Area # 6

Description
Dense spiderwebs cover the stalactites and stalagmites and there are some spots of yellow ooze on the flowstones. There’s something lying on the floor.
  • There's a poisonous darts trap here, hidden inside shafts in the wall. When undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through its trip wire. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • Lying on the floor is a rotting orc corpse. Searching it will uncover:
    • 25 gp in coins
    • 350 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): jasper, Bloodstone, Carnelian, 2 × Moonstone, Zircon, Citrine
    • Magic Items: Potion of clairvoyance, Potion of mind reading, Potion of superior healing, Scroll of protection
    • Mirror (hand-sized, steel) (Unusable)
    • Oil (Unusable)
  • There’s also an eyeball in a sealed jar near the wall on the left and some broken arrows next to it.
