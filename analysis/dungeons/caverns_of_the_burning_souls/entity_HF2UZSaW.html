Cave area #5 in Caverns of the Burning Souls

Foreshadowing
  • A few single copper pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Description
There are stains of what seems to be blood on the ground and the ground is covered with mossy patches. There is a large barrel near the far wall.
  • The barrel is trapped. There's a small gas-filled blister made of lizard's intestine hidden in a rim. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the barrel contains:
    • 212 gp in coins
    • 40 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Obsidian, Hematite, Malachite, Banded agate
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a ring of fist-sized stones near the wall on the left and some carved figurines spread all over the place.
