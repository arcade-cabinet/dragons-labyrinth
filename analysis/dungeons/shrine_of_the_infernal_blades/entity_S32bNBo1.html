Foreshadowing
  • A successful listening roll could detect the sounds that the Young Red Dragon is making inside this area.
Doorways
  • W side - arched bronze door (Barricaded)

Description
There are stains of what seems to be blood all over the place and the entryway has some cracks on it. There’s movement inside.
  • There’s a Young Red Dragon inside. It will attack anyone stepping in.
