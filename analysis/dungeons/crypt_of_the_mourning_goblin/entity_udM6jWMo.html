Description
An altar decorated with figurines of horned demons stands here and the entryway is covered with lichen growths. There is a big jar near the far wall.
  • The jar is trapped. Loose debris is locked above the jar, released when a switch embedded in a handle is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jar contains:
    • 211 gp in coins
    • 125 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Carved bone statuette, Cloth-of-gold vestments, Pair of engraved bone dice, Silver ewer, Small mirror set in a painted wooden frame
    • Magic Items: 2 × Horseshoes of a zephyr, Potion of speed, Potion of supreme healing, Scroll6th of Magic Jar (Wizard)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a figure made of sticks and twine on an altar and some tiny mushrooms spread all over the place.
  • The words "Wondrous Traitor" in Chaotic symbols were inscribed on wooden plaque fixed to a wall. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 58 (to the W)
