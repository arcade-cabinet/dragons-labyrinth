Description
It is dark and damp here and the floor has a few fractures on it. There’s a dead body here.
  • Lying on the floor is the dust covered skeleton of a goblin. Searching it will uncover:
    • 27 gp in coins
    • 175 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): 2 × Black velvet mask stitched with silver thread, 4 × Embroidered silk handkerchief, Silver ewer
  • There’s also a rusty short sword on an altar and some thick spiderwebs next to it.
  • There’s a pressure plate lever that can open the secret door in area 6 (to the W)
  • A plain looking wooden chest contains Trystane of Headsmen’s from Headsmen custom-fit armor (actually an Armour +1 )
