Foreshadowing
  • A few single silver pieces could be found if examining the ground near the entrances to this area.
Doorways
  • S side - arched bronze door (Trapped)
    • This doorway is magically trapped. When undetected using a DC18 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger when crossing. Succeed a DC20 Intelligence saving throw or forget how to cast one random spell for one day.
  • W side - triangle wooden door (Half-broken)
  • N side - arched bronze door (Barricaded)
  • E side - arched marble door (Stuck)
Description
It is dark and damp here and the hidden alcoves are covered with spots of yellow ooze. There are some big jars near the far wall.
  • The jars are trapped. There's a small gas-filled vial made of an extremely fragile glass embedded in a handle. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jars contain:
    • 207 gp in coins
    • 20 gp worth of gemstones (10gp each): Turquoise, Blue quartz
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a pile of papers near the wall on the left and some carved figurines next to it.
  • Searching will uncover an old leather pouch holding the key (🜂) to unlock the arched bronze door (to the W) in area 79
