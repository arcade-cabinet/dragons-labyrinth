Description
The air in this area is dense and misty and the floor is covered with scorch marks. There are some small crates near the far wall.
  • The crates are trapped. There's a poisoned needle embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC17 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will prick the greedy victim. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crates contain:
    • 170 gp in coins
    • 250 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): 2 × Sardonyx, jasper, 2 × Quartz
    • Magic Items: Blowgun needles (+2), Potion of diminution, 2 × Potion of superior healing
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a small empty bottle in a large basin and some shreded pages of a book next to it.
