Foreshadowing
  • Traces of something heavy dragged on the ground could be found if examining around the entrances to this area.
Doorways
  • E side - passage to area 35.
Description
A sealed wooden sarcophagus is laying here and there are a few tufts of fungus on the corner sides. There are some very big chests near the far wall.
  • The chests are trapped. There's a small gas-filled vial made of an extremely fragile glass hidden under a pulling ring. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC20 Constitution saving throw or sustain 1d6 turns of uncontrolled spasms.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the chests contain:
    • 162 gp in coins
    • 150 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Carnelian, Chrysoprase, Citrine
    • Magic Items: Potion of supreme healing, Scroll6th of Circle of Death (Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a spotted mushroom on an altar and some broken pieces of wood spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a nook in the wall holding the key (🝂) to unlock the arched wooden door (to the W) in area 48
  • The words "Dead Flames" in Neutral symbols were inscribed on wooden plaque fixed to a wall. It is the passphrase for the secret door in area 31 (to the W)
