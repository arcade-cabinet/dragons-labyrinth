Description
Three or four half-broken sarcophagi are laying here and the hidden alcoves are covered with spots of yellow ooze. There is a big crate near the far wall.
  • The crate is trapped. There's a small gas-filled blister made of goat's intestine embedded in one of the wooden planks. If undetected using a DC16 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will pop open. Succeed a DC15 Constitution saving throw or die in 3d4 turns.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the crate contains:
    • 240 gp in coins
    • 100 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Bloodstone, Zircon
    • Magic Items: Necklace of fireballs, Potion of clairvoyance, Potion of fire giant strength, Scroll4th of Freedom of Movement (Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin)
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
