Foreshadowing
  • A successful listening roll could detect the sounds that the Salamanders are making inside this area.

Description
It is dark and damp here and there are several cracks on the rock faces. A person is chained to the wall and there’s movement inside.
  • There are 4 Salamanders inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.
