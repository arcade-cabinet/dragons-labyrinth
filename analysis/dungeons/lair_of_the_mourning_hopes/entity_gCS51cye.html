Foreshadowing
  • There's a good chance players could sense the vile stench that the Gray Oozes are emitting from inside this area.
  • A successful listen roll will also reveal screams of fear coming from inside this area.
Description
This area stinks from excrements and the ground is covered with burn marks. A person is chained to the wall and a strange sensation is creeping up your spine.
  • There are 5 Gray Oozes inside. They will attack anyone stepping in.
