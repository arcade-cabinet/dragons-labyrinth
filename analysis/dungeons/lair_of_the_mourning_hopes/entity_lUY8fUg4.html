Foreshadowing
  • It is impossible to hear anything inside.
Description
Dense spiderwebs are stretched between cave columns and there are several fractures on the passages. You feel as if you're being watched.
  • Gazuzar the Ancient Red Dragon is inside this area. It will attack anyone stepping in.

If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the dragon's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.
