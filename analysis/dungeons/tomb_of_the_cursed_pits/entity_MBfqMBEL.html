  • S side - arched bronze door (Trapped)

Description
There are several reading alcoves here and the corner sides have several cracks on them. There is a large bag near the far wall.
  • The bag is trapped. Loose rubble is locked above the bag, released when a switch thrown casually inside is triggered. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active), it will drop down. Succeed in a DC15 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the bag contains:
    • 3,606 gp in coins
    • 550 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Onyx, 2 × Sardonyx, Bloodstone, 2 × Carnelian, Chrysoprase, Star rose quartz, Moonstone, Zircon, Citrine
    • Magic Items: Potion of cloud giant strength, Potion of longevity, Potion of speed
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a tangle of vines near the wall on the right and some hundred tiny bones next to it.
  • Searching will uncover a cloth wrap under a stone holding the key (🜓) to unlock the rectangular bronze door (to the W) in area 24
