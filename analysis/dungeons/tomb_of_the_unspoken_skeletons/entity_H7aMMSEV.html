Description
The air in this area is dense and misty and the hidden alcoves have several tufts of fungus on them. There is a big jar near the far wall.
  • There’s a 2-in-6 chance the jar contains:
    • 4,208 gp in coins
    • 550 gp worth of gemstones (50gp each): Onyx, jasper, 2 × Chalcedony, Star rose quartz, Moonstone, Zircon, 4 × Citrine
    Otherwise, this place was already looted earlier.
  • There’s also a carved idol below an arch and some broken arrows spread all over the place.
  • Searching will uncover a crack in the floor holding the key (🝓) to unlock the rectangular wooden door (to the S) in area 1
