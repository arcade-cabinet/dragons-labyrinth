Description
Various torturing devices are fixtured here and the hidden alcoves have a few mossy patches on them. You spot something quite big lying on the floor.
  • There's a crossbow trap here, hidden inside a niche in the wall. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping on pressure plate. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 2d4 damage from the fast and sharp arrow.
  • Lying on the floor is a dust covered human skeleton. Searching it will uncover:
    • 28 gp in coins
    • 50 gp worth of artifacts (25gp each): Copper chalice with silver filigree, Small mirror set in a painted wooden frame
    • Magic Items: Blowgun needles (+1), Potion of hill giant strength, Potion of resistance
    • Rations (iron, 7 days) (Unusable)
    • Club (Unusable)
    • Hammer (small) (Usable)
  • A broken wooden box can be found in the near corner.
