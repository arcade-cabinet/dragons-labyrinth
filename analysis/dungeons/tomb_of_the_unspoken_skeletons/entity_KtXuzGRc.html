Foreshadowing
  • It is impossible to hear anything inside.
  • A successful listen roll will also reveal screams of fear coming from inside this area.
Doorways
  • S side - round iron door (Barricaded)
  • N side - rectangular wooden door (Half-broken)
  • W side - rectangular bronze door (Trapped)
    • There's a giant blade locked inside a cavity above the door. When undetected using a DC15 Wisdom (Perception, passive or active) check, it will trigger if stepping through an almost invisible trip wire. Succeed a DC20 Dexterity saving throw or sustain 1d8 damage.
  • E side - passage to area 18.
Description
Chains and shackles are fixtured all around and the walls are heavily weathered. A person is chained to the wall and you feel the chill of death curling up your spine.
  • Bazakor the Vampire is inside this area. It will attack anyone stepping in.
