## Playthrough Comparison

**Bandit Cave (Strength) vs Lost Child (Harmony)**:

**Mechanical Differences**:
- Combat tutorial vs Environmental navigation
- Trait focus: Warrior/Shadow vs Protector/Cooperative
- Companion: None vs Whisper (fog specialist)
- Health: Damaged vs Full
- Items: Combat loot vs Mysterious artifacts

**Narrative Differences**:
- Dragon seen as: Corrupting force vs Possible guardian
- Town reaction: Celebrated hero vs Unsettling harbinger
- World changes: Basic unease vs Fog corruption
- Knowledge: Dragon cultists exist vs Dragon might be protecting us

**Emotional Journey**:
- Bandit Cave: Last normal adventure → growing wrongness
- Lost Child: Immediate supernatural → emotional horror

**What This Reveals**:
The game has two completely different onboarding experiences that fundamentally change how you perceive the dragon and your role in the world. The Harmony path introduces supernatural elements immediately but suggests the dragon might not be evil, while the Strength path maintains "normal" adventure longer but shows human corruption.
